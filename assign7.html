Advanced Genres

EDM
Energetic beats and the way it creates an exciting atmosphere. The powerful drops and fast rhythms make it perfect for dancing and getting people hyped up.
Rock
Strong guitar riffs and expressive sound. It has a lot of energy and emotion, which makes it fun to listen to whether you're relaxing or looking for something intense.
Metal
Stands out for its heavy guitars and powerful drums that create an intense and dramatic sound. It can be aggressive and loud, but it also has a lot of creativity and skill behind the music.
